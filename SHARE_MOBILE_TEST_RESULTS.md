## Known Limitations

### Share Image Preview
The share image is generated correctly by `ShareManager.generateShareImage()` with:
- 1080x1920 resolution (Instagram story size)
- Game branding ("FLAPPY DON" text)
- Score prominently displayed
- Medal emoji (🥉🥈🥇💎)
- Character emoji (😐/🙂/😎 based on score)
- "Download Flappy Don" call-to-action
- American flag emoji (🇺🇸)

However, the iOS share sheet preview shows a generic document icon instead of rendering the full custom image. This is expected iOS behavior and does not indicate a bug. The actual image will be shared correctly when the user selects a destination app (Messages, Mail, Instagram, etc.).
